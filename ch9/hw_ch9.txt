Q5. Yes we can make a version of the graph present in the book for this scenario. We should observe a decrease in fairness as the value of quantum is increased for the above scenario. 
Another thing that we can explore is the combined effect of increase in job length and quantum length on the fairness of scheduler. I think if the job length increases to say 10000 from 100 the effect of increase in quantum lenght from 1 to 5 will not be that prominent.
Stride scheduler should be equally fair for the above scenario with two jobs of length 100 and 100 ticjets, independent of quantum length. Stride scheduler will alternate between the two processes for quantum length and hence it is independent of quantum.
